# ЁЯПи Hotel Revenue & Performance Analytics

## ЁЯУМ Project Overview

This project analyzes hotel operational, financial, and guest-related data to evaluate revenue performance, occupancy trends, booking behavior, cost structure, and overall profitability.

The goal is to generate actionable business insights using data-driven analysis and key performance indicators (KPIs).

---

## ЁЯОп Objectives

- Analyze revenue performance across time and market segments
- Evaluate occupancy trends and seasonality
- Compare booking channels and guest types

- Provide strategic recommendations for revenue optimization

## ЁЯУК Business Insights

- Revenue peaks during high season and holidays.
- Weekends often generate higher ADR.
- OTA channels increase booking volume but reduce margins.
- Leisure segment contributes significantly during peak seasons.
- High cancellations and no-shows reduce effective revenue.
- Revenue management improves segment-specific RevPAR.

---

## ЁЯТб Strategic Recommendations

- Increase direct bookings to reduce OTA commission costs.
- Implement dynamic pricing strategies.
- Optimize marketing spend based on ROI.
- Reduce cancellations through policy adjustments.
- Focus on high-profit segments.
- Improve guest satisfaction to increase repeat bookings.
